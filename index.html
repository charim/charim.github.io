CHARIM KOREAN RESTAURANT

ABOUT

Charim [Cha-reem]

The name Charim meaning ‘set’ or ‘the table is set’ represents our placement in what we believe is the basis of healthy traditional Korean food. Located in the St. Matthews area of Louisville, KY, Charim seeks to gather people of all backgrounds sharing the traditional Korean dining experience. The chef’s goal is to serve healthy, fresh, as well as delicious food by adding no chemicals such as MSG and also providing gluten-free foods. We hope to represent a positive image of Korea and to create an enjoyable dining experience for everyone who comes to our restaurant. Charim would like to create a sharing and tasting experience to take you and your companions on a culinary tour of Korea. Thank you for allowing us to invite and serve you at Charim.
